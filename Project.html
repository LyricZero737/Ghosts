What is a Ghost?

Ghosts are dead souls with unfinished business. They are made of ecto-plasm.

Types of Ghost:

  • Lost-Only around to finish unfinished business
  • Confused-Don't remember themselves and often hide from humans
  • Malicious-Run and don't look back

What to Do?

  1. Run with friends, pets and loved ones out of area till you know it's safe
  2. Pray that you are indeed safe from the ghosts
  3. Call Ghost busters,Or your local priest.
  4. Wait and see if the situation is resovled
  5. If situation is unresolved. Your out of luck and should just move out and cut your losses.
